Why History?

Well, history is part of a bigger group of subjects known as Social Science. Most parts of Social Science tell us about
the world in the present but history helps us understand how the present has evolved. It tells us about the

Can you, for instance, think of a society in which cultivation of crops was unknown? Can you imagine life
without fire or electricity? Or, what it was to live at a time when roads and railways did not exist,
yet people travelled long distances? History can take us into these pasts. History can open doors to such worlds.
In this sense, history is an adventure. It is a journey across time and space.
